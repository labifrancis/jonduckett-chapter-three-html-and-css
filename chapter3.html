    • Scrambled Eggs

    Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled egss

    Ingreedients

    • 2 eggs
    • 1 tbs butter
    • 2tbs cream

    Method

    1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
    2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
    3. Once butter has melted add eggs and cream
    4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the span to the to the center every 2o seconds (as if you're making an omletter)
    5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook in the plate until served)
